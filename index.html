About Us

Hotel Shingar Manali is a budget hotel near Mall Road, offering great hospitality and comfortable stay amidst hot and happening city beautiful Manali. It is comfortable located near mall road, walking distance from the main bus stand and taxi stand. Rooms at the hotel are well furnished along with all the modern amenities. Guest can enjoy the city life of mall road Manali, enjoy the late night buzz, city food etc. Hotel with the basic aim of setting high standards in the field of hospitality. Hotel is committed towards high quality services at a reasonable tariff structure, good view, good food & services... The warm hospitality and comfortable stay awaits for you at Hotel Shingar Manali. .
